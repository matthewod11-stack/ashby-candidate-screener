## About Acme AI

Acme AI is a fast-growing startup building AI-powered software for modern teams. The company is scaling quickly, and the backend systems powering that growth need dedicated technical leadership.

---

## The Role

This is a technical leadership position where you'll own and evolve the platform's backbone. You'll shape architecture, set engineering standards, and make long-term infrastructure decisions that determine how the platform scales. You'll work directly with founders and product leads, balancing speed with sustainability in a fast-moving startup environment where your work ships quickly and influences the entire product.

---

## What You'll Do

- **Architect and scale core systems** — Design, build, and own the backend services that power the product, from API layer through data pipeline
- **Lead authentication and identity infrastructure** — Own SSO, SAML, and OIDC integrations, ensuring enterprise-grade auth that scales with the customer base
- **Set technical direction** — Define system design patterns, service boundaries, data modeling approaches, and long-term infrastructure strategy
- **Mentor and elevate the team** — Raise the bar through design reviews, pairing, knowledge-sharing, and establishing best practices across backend engineering
- **Drive reliability and observability** — Proactively identify bottlenecks, improve performance, and evolve infrastructure before problems become incidents

---

## What We're Looking For

### Required

- 6+ years of backend engineering experience with a track record of operating at Staff level or as a technical lead on complex, production systems
- Deep expertise in **SSO, SAML, and OIDC** — you've built or owned identity and authentication systems, not just consumed them
- Broad understanding of **identity provider (IdP) architecture** and enterprise auth patterns
- Strong proficiency in **Python** with experience in **FastAPI** or similar modern frameworks
- Deep knowledge of **distributed systems design**, database architecture, and scaling strategies
- Hands-on experience with **SQL and NoSQL databases** (e.g., Postgres, Supabase, SQLAlchemy)
- Strong command of **cloud infrastructure** (AWS: EC2, S3, RDS, Lambda) and **containerization** (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Comfort operating in a fast-paced startup where priorities shift and impact is immediate
- Experience with **entitlements management**, **usage-based metering**, or **billing systems** (e.g., Stripe Billing, Lago, or custom metering pipelines)
